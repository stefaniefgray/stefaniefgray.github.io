What do I bring to the table?

Intellectual curiosity has led me down many paths: data analysis, web development, research, writing, campaign strategy, and cartographic design. In turn, I consider myself a 'geek-of-all-trades.'

I'm currently in the Product and Design track at the Startup Institute, bolstering my skills in front-end web development, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, UI/UX.

What's next? Let's talk about it! I'll be taking my new skills to a company that will want a person with this passion and know-how! I want to share my love of technology and build a product that benefits society and makes people happy.
